== Description ==

<strong>Contact Form 7 Multi-Step</strong> helps you add multi-step for your form.<br>
This is the best solution to keep the form as simple as possible to your visitors.

<strong>LIVE DEMO:</strong> https://demo.ninjateam.org/contact-form-7-multi-step/

<h4>FEATURES</h4>

<ul>
<li>MAKE YOUR FORM LOOK BETTER WITH MULTI-STEP</li>
<li>EASY TO ADD STEP TAG TO YOUR FORM WITH A CLICK</li>
<li>EASY CUSTOM YOUR BUTTON FOR EACH STEP</li>
<li>UNLIMITED STEPS</li>
</ul>

<h4>HOW IT WORKS</h4>
<ul>
<li>Fully customizable no matter how you want to change it to fit your requirements</li>
<li>Simple front-end user interface makes it quick to familiarize yourself with the plugin</li>
<li>Useful documentation available at hand to resolve any issues you may encounter</li>
<li>A tag in Contact Form 7 allowing you to sort emails out based on their importance</li>
</ul>

<h4>INSTALLATION & SETUP</h4>
Manual installation is easy and takes less than one minute.
<ul>
 	<li>Download the plugin from wordpress.org, unpack it and upload the <strong>[Contact Form 7 Multi-Step]</strong> folder to your <strong>wp-content/plugins/</strong> directory.</li>
 	<li>Activate the plugin through the '<strong>Plugins</strong>' menu in WordPress.</li>
 	<li>Go to your Contact Form in <strong>Wordpress left menu &gt; Contact </strong>to add step tag and click <strong>Save Changes</strong> button.</li>
 	<li>Done. Enjoy.</li>
</ul>

Note: If you don't have Contact Form 7 just yet. You can download <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-7/">here</a>


== Installation ==
Manual installation is easy and takes fewer than one minute.

1. Download the plugin from wordpress.org, unpack it and upload the <strong>[Contact Form 7 Multi-Step]</strong> folder to your <strong>wp-content/plugins/</strong> directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins‘ menu in WordPress.
3. Go to your Contact Form in <strong>Wordpress left menu &gt; Contact </strong>to add step tag and click <strong>Save Changes</strong> button.

You’re done. Enjoy.
